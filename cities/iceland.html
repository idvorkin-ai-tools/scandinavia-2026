Iceland

Reykjavík & Hafnarfjörður base · the opening leg
MapReykjavík on Google Maps ↗

Two short nights to kick off the trip — base near Reykjavík and aim the daylight at waterfalls, craters, and a lava cave.

The nature stuff is the headline. Chase waterfalls — Brúarfoss, the bluest of them all, sits about 90–120 minutes from Reykjavík with just a 5–10 minute walk from the lot, and slots in neatly between the Geysir geothermal area and Laugarvatn/Þingvellir if you string a loop together. Beyond it: Skógafoss and Goðafoss for the big curtains of water, Fjaðrárgljúfur Canyon for the carved-river view, the ice chunks scattered on the black sand at Diamond Beach, and if there's appetite for a detour, Víti Crater and the Valley of Tears. With only two nights you won't get all of these — pick a direction and commit.

Make Hafnarfjörður, the little town about 15 minutes outside Reykjavík, the home base. Café Barbara for coffee, Sól for dinner inside an actual greenhouse, and a swim, the pond, and the seaside all within reach. For the one big outing, the Raufarhólshellir lava caves are an easy 1–3 hour trip with pickup right in Reykjavík — bring warm and waterproof layers plus a hat, it's cold and drippy down there. And graze the Icelandic street food between stops.

The pick-list

  • Brúarfoss — the bluest waterfall; ~90–120 min from Reykjavík, 5–10 min walk from the parking lot. Good stop between the Geysir geothermal area and Laugarvatn/Þingvellir.
  • Skógafoss — classic broad south-coast waterfall.
  • Goðafoss — the "waterfall of the gods."
  • Fjaðrárgljúfur Canyon — dramatic river-carved gorge.
  • Diamond Beach — ice chunks on black sand.
  • Víti Crater — volcanic crater lake.
  • Valley of Tears — scenic detour.
  • Hafnarfjörður (town ~15 min outside Reykjavík) — base for the leg: Café Barbara (coffee), Sól (restaurant in a greenhouse), a spot to swim, the pond, and the seaside.
  • Icelandic street food — graze between stops.
  • Raufarhólshellir lava caves — pickup in Reykjavík; 1–3 hrs; easy. Bring warm + waterproof clothes and a hat.
