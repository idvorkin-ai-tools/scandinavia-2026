Aurland

Norway · the fjord heart — Aurlandsfjord & Nærøyfjord
MapAurland on Google Maps ↗

This is the slow, spectacular middle of the trip — two nights to stop moving and just let the fjords be enormous around you.

The headline here is the Fjordsafari RIB boat trip: a small, fast inflatable that runs out across Aurlandsfjord and into the UNESCO-listed Nærøyfjord — the narrowest, most dramatic arm of the whole system. It's roughly 2h 15m, with flotation suits handed out at the dock so the wind and spray don't matter. Up top, the Stegastein viewpoint juts a wood-and-glass platform 30 stories of air above the water. This leg isn't about ticking boxes — it's for waterfalls, sheer green walls, and quiet.

Flåm sits right next door, so the famous Flåm Railway (Flåmsbana) — one of the world's great scenic rail rides — and the little fjord-end village are both within easy reach.
