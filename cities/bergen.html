Bergen

Norway · Bryggen, the funiculars & the fjord gateway
MapBergen on Google Maps ↗

Three nights in Norway's rainy, wooden, sea-salt city — where colorful wharf gables meet two mountains and the front door to the fjords.

Start at the waterline: Bryggen Wharf, the crooked row of Hanseatic gabled houses, then the open-air fish market right beside it. Do a walking tour to get your bearings, then just wander the narrow streets and alleys behind the wharf. Work in the Bergenhus Fortress, St Mary's Church (Bergen's oldest building), the KODE art museums, and Old Bergen (Gamle Bergen) for the open-air historic-town feel. Then go up high — two mountains frame the city. Ride the Fløibanen funicular (the sideways tram) up to Mt Fløyen for the classic city view — trolls and goats up top — and take the Ulriken gondola to Mt Ulriken, the highest of the seven mountains, with hiking trails and scenic viewpoints. For legs that want to earn the view, climb the Stoltzekleiven stairs.

Bergen is the gateway to the fjords, so give a full day to a fjord tour — a Sognefjord boat cruise threading waterfalls and tiny village stops is the headline outing. Back in town, eat simply: Trekroneren for a quick hot dog / sausage, then linger at a harbor-sunset cafe. Catch an English-language comedy show one evening for a break from sightseeing. And just outside town, the dark-timbered Fantoft Stave Church is an easy add-on.

The pick-list

  • City & waterfront — Bryggen Wharf · the fish market · explore the narrow streets · harbor walk · Bergenhus Fortress · St Mary's Church · a walking tour
  • Mountains & views — Fløibanen funicular (the sideways tram) up to Mt Fløyen — city view, plus trolls and goats · Ulriken gondola / cable car to Mt Ulriken · hiking and trails · scenic viewpoints · climb the Stoltzekleiven stairs
  • Fjord tour — Sognefjord boat cruise / fjord experience · waterfalls · village stops (Bergen = the fjord gateway)
  • Food & culture — Trekroneren (the hot dog / sausage spot) · cafes and sunsets by the harbor · KODE museum · Old Bergen (Gamle Bergen) · an English comedy show · Fantoft Stave Church just outside Bergen
