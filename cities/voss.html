Voss

Norway · adventure town between fjord & Bergen
MapVoss on Google Maps ↗

One night to catch your breath between the fjords and Bergen — ride the gondola up for the view, then keep moving.

Voss is the breather stop: a short layover with one clear headline and a couple of easy add-ons if the legs still have energy. Honest note: Igor hasn't filled this one in yet, so the three picks below are a seed, not a plan — but they're the things worth doing with a single night here.

Voss Gondola (Voss Gondol)

The headline. The cable car lifts you straight from the train and bus station up to Mount Hanguren at ~820 m in under ten minutes, opening onto a sweeping look over the valley, the lake, and the surrounding peaks. Figure roughly a 1.5-hour round trip if you just ride up, take in the view, and come back down.

It runs year-round, so even a quick stop works — but if you have time, the summit station is the trailhead for short walks and a café, making it an easy way to trade train-seat stiffness for fresh air and altitude.

More details ↗🕑 Hours: open year-round; summer hours run long (typically late morning to evening) but vary by season & weather · verify before you go

Adventure sports

Voss bills itself as Norway's adventure-sports capital, and a night here is enough to fit in one thrill. Voss Active, running since 1992, is the main outfitter — white-water rafting, family rafting, canyoning, and a high-rope and zip-line park up the valley (the longest zip crosses a river at over 150 m). Paragliding and kayaking also run out of the area if rafting isn't your speed.

Most activities need booking ahead and a half-day, so this is the "if a night feels like enough for a jolt of adrenaline" option — skip it if you'd rather just ride the gondola and rest.

More details ↗🕑 Hours: seasonal (summer rafting/zip-line season); book ahead, half-day slots · verify before you go

Voss as a Bergen Line rail stop

Practically, Voss is a station on the Bergen Line (Bergensbanen) — the gateway between Flåm/Aurland and Bergen. From here it's a straightforward, direct ride of about an hour into Bergen, with the gondola, town, and station all within steps of each other.

That makes Voss an easy in-and-out: arrive from the fjords, do the gondola, sleep, and roll on to Bergen the next morning. Check Vy for live times and tickets.

🔍 Mini Scavenger Hunt
  • Ride the Voss Gondola from the station up to Hangurstoppen, 820 m above the valley
  • Spot Lake Vangsvatnet stretching from Bulken all the way to Voss town centre
  • Find Tvindefossen waterfall and walk the short trail right up to its 110 m base
  • Spot the old stone church, Vangskyrkja, in the town centre — it dates back to 1277!
  • Hike or peek into the dramatic Bordalsgjelet gorge with its tall rock walls
  • Spot a sheep or goat on the mountainsides above town
  • Count the waterfalls you can see streaming down the slopes around Voss
