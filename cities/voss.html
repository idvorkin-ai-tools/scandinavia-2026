Voss

Norway · adventure town between fjord & Bergen
MapVoss on Google Maps ↗

One night to catch your breath between the fjords and Bergen — ride the gondola up for the view, then keep moving.

The headline here is the Voss Gondola (Voss Gondol), which climbs from the lakeside station to a ~820 m summit for a sweeping look over the valley — roughly a 1.5-hour round trip if you just go up and back. Beyond that, Voss is Norway's adventure-sports capital: rafting, kayaking, paragliding, and zip-lines all run out of town, so it's an easy place to swap a day of sitting on trains for a jolt of altitude. Practically, it's a stop on the Bergen Line railway — the gateway between Aurland/Flåm and Bergen. Honest note: Igor hasn't filled this stop in yet, so the list below is a seed, not a plan.
