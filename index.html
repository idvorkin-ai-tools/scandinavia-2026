Reykjavík

explainer

The tiny capital on a volcanic hinge — Viking settlement, Danish rule, WWII, NATO, and ground that's still under construction.
🇩🇰

Copenhagen

explainer

A royal harbor capital rebuilt after fires and bombardment — a merchant city turned welfare-state showcase of design, bikes, and food.
🇸🇪

Stockholm

explainer

A capital built on a choke point between Lake Mälaren and the Baltic — medieval control, Baltic power politics, and design-minded civic order.
🇳🇴

Oslo

explainer

Not Copenhagen with mountains — a compact capital where forest, monarchy, maritime trade, oil wealth, and social democracy collide.
⛰️

Voss

explainer

The hinge between fjord spectacle and inland mountain life — railway town, outdoor-sports base, farming valley, and festival stop.
🌉

Bergen

explainer

Norway's old great trading city — a Hanseatic stockfish hub and rainy North Sea harbor, now western Norway's cultural capital.
🇳🇱

Amsterdam

explainer

A planned water-management city that became a 17th-century trade superpower — now wrestling with tourism, housing, and success.
